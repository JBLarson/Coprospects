About LiveWire Solutions Electricians

Your Trusted, Locally-Owned Electrical Experts in Southern California.

Our Story: Lighting Up SoCal Since 2005

LiveWire Solutions Electricians was founded with a simple mission: to provide Southern California with safe, reliable, and high-quality electrical services. For over 15 years, we've been dedicated to keeping homes and businesses powered, safe, and up-to-date with the latest electrical standards and technologies.

What started as a one-man operation has grown into a trusted team of certified, experienced electricians who share a passion for problem-solving and customer satisfaction. We believe in honest work, transparent pricing, and building lasting relationships with our clients. We're not just electricians; we're your neighbors, committed to the safety and well-being of our community.
